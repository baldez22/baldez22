About the Artist

JossyArtz is a passionate Kenyan fine artist renowned for his expertise in pencil art, specializing in graphite, charcoal as well as latte art. He brings imaginations to life through drawing stunning portraits, music-inspired art, soccer illustrations, charcoal art, wildlife and nature arts and even latte art. Ranked among the top artists by an African art hub (MASK), his talent has also gained international recognition, with artworks like his Messi-Argentina World Cup winning piece receiving global appreciation. Beyond art, JossyArtz is a graduate in Tourism Management, has a keen eye for web development, an agriculturist, and an insightful football analyst who stays updated with the latest trends. He is also proficient in remote tasks, leveraging his sharp analytical mind to adapt, strategize, and make the best out of every situation. His diverse expertise blends creativity, technical skills, and deep intellectual insight, making him a dynamic and multifaceted professional.

Drawings

This striking pencil artwork blends realism with expressive color to create a powerful portrait. The subject's intense gaze and bold features are accentuated by a vivid mix of hues, giving the piece a dynamic and almost electric feel. The deep black shading in the hair and beard contrasts beautifully with the vibrant skin tones, adding depth and personality. A unique fusion of traditional portraiture and contemporary artistic style.

The art "For the love", is a stunning pencil drawing depicting Jesus Christ wearing a crown of thorns, gazing down solemnly at the scene of the crucifixion. The lower portion of the artwork illustrates three crosses, symbolizing the crucifixion of Jesus and the two thieves at Golgotha. The shading and intricate details, especially in the facial expression and texture of the hair and thorns, evoke deep emotion, love for the people and spirituality. The contrast between light and dark enhances the dramatic effect of the composition.

This is a stunning colored pencil portrait of a woman wearing a vibrant headscarf and large hoop earrings. The artwork captures intricate facial details, rich textures, and expressive eyes that exude confidence and grace. The shading and highlights bring depth to her skin tone, while the headscarf adds a splash of color, beautifully contrasting with her natural curls. The background softly fades, ensuring focus remains on the subject. The piece showcases an impressive blend of realism and artistic expression.

This is a beautifully detailed pencil portrait of a young girl, skillfully rendered in grayscale. The artwork captures her expressive eyes, delicate facial features, and natural hair texture with impressive precision. The shading and highlights add depth, making the portrait look lifelike. This piece showcases remarkable realism and artistic mastery.
